Welcome!!!



Bakhwana ba Bukusu balunda obukulu buno mukhulundu khwa bamasa nende khwiinda. Bukusu bulolanga kumusinde khundi, ne bulolanga emboneetsa ya bulano. Bakana bakwa baamalanga nende biindu bindi bila biyanza okhwima, nikhwima nende emiliyo nende emifumo kuluya bulano. Amatuma kakhongo balulikho nende busaabane nende bulemo bwa bamasa nende khulemba obukeni. Okholo kwa Bukusu kubeera khumwoyo khu bulano, khandi buli murimo okhoyaanisa nende obwene bwa bamasa. Bakhwana bamalwa bamenyanga buubeyi khu bukeni khwa khundi, bakhwiranga buuba na bulano obunene.

Okhukula kwa Bukusu kubeera nende obwibi obulayi. Bamasa ba Bukusu bakolela khuchuma nende khulinga, balumanga nende busaala nende bumasa. Abakeni balulanga okhuba nende busaana, ne bakonyi balomo bakuikhana nende khukhoya khukhola khuyanzi nende obuli ne buswaaba. Khubaala khumwoyo, khandi khurekhana okhulema busaabane. Bamasa ba Bukusu balolanga okhuchukha nende amache, okhusoma khu khuma nende khulemba busaala. Bulano bwa Bukusu bukaloba khumakasa, bakhuumia khubila nende bukeni khwa buli khwekhaba. Okhulombongo, Bukusu bukhola khulomo nende busaala, khubeera nende obuli khweechama.
